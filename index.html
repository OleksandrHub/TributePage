Steve Jobs

A great visionary, entrepreneur and father of the digital revolution.

Timeline of Steve Jobs' Life

  • 1955: Born on February 24 in San Francisco, California.
  • 1972: Enrolled at Reed College, dropped out after six months.
  • 1974: Worked at Atari and traveled to India.
  • 1976: Co-founded Apple Inc. with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne.
  • 1984: Launched the Macintosh computer.
  • 1985: Left Apple after a power struggle.
  • 1985: Founded NeXT Inc.
  • 1986: Purchased The Graphics Group, later known as Pixar.
  • 1996: Apple acquired NeXT; Jobs returned to Apple.
  • 1997: Became interim CEO of Apple, later CEO.
  • 2001: Launched the iPod.
  • 2007: Introduced the iPhone
  • 2010: Released the iPad.
  • 2011: Resigned as CEO of Apple due to health issues.
  • 2011: Passed away on October 5 at the age of 56
